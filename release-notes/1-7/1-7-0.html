Release Notes

A new version of New Tab Tweaker has been installed.

What's new in 1.7.0
  • Adds the option to show top sites: you can either have them be always visible, or only visible after a click anywhere inside a new tab. If you prefer not to see your top sites at all, that is still possible.
  • Adds two new wallpaper scaling options:
    • None will display the wallpaper in its original dimensions, regardless of window resolution.
    • Automatic scaling takes into account the wallpaper's original dimensions and the window's current resolution and tries to select the most appropriate scaling (either 'fit', 'fill' or 'none') based on that.
  • Redesigns the new tab's user interface to be more touch friendly: the customization button is revealed by clicking anywhere inside a new tab.
